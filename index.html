Entry checklist
✓Real catalyst confirmed — googled and verified
✓Float under 20M — lower is better
✓Relative volume 2x+ — real buying interest
✓Price above VWAP — momentum confirmed
✓Clean pullback to support — not chasing the spike
✓Stop loss set — know your exit before entering
✓Calculator run — position sized, risk under $50
✓Emotional state checked — calm and confident only
Exit checklist
✓Stop hit → exit immediately, no argument
✓Target hit → take at least partial profit
✓Volume fading + price stalling → consider exit
✓Price breaks below key support → exit
✓Loss approaching $50 → exit, no averaging down
✓After 10:30 and momentum gone → exit
✓Log the trade immediately after exit

Mental rules
→Missed trade ≠ bad trader. The next setup is always coming.
→FOMO from one miss causes bad entries on the next setup. Reset between trades.
→Cutting a loss quickly is not a bad trade — it's discipline.
→Singles add up. Consistent wins build the account.
→The stock doesn't know you own it. It doesn't owe you a recovery.
→1% better every day. Compound the improvement, not just the money.
