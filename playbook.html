Playbook
Rules, routines, order types. Know these cold.
Daily rules
1Cut losses quickly — always. No exceptions.
2Only trade stocks with a real catalyst — news, deal, FDA, earnings beat.
3Set stop loss before entering — always. Know your exit before your entry.
4Max loss per trade: $50. Use the Calculator first.
5Primary window: 9:30–10:30am. After 10:30 only if volume + catalyst + VWAP confirm.
6No averaging down — ever.
7Never underestimate a stock. Movement creates movement.
8Never trust a stock. It doesn't owe you anything.
9Log every trade in the journal — win or lose.
10No trade day is still a good day. Patience is a position.
Morning routine
7:00Benzinga pre-market gainers — top 3-5 movers.
7:15Google catalyst on anything up 20%+. Real news only.
7:30Pull charts. Identify support, resistance, VWAP.
8:00Scanner on. Confirm volume building on watchlist.
9:20Watchlist locked. Calculator run on each setup.
9:30Market open — watch first 5-10 min. Do not trade immediately.
9:40First clean pullback setup = entry window opens.
Entry checklist
✓Real catalyst confirmed — googled and verified
✓Float under 20M — lower is better
✓Relative volume 2x+ — real buying interest
✓Price above VWAP — momentum confirmed
✓Clean pullback to support — not chasing the spike
✓Stop loss set — know your exit before entering
✓Calculator run — position sized, risk under $50
✓Emotional state checked — calm and confident only
Exit checklist
✓Stop hit → exit immediately, no argument
✓Target hit → take at least partial profit
✓Volume fading + price stalling → consider exit
✓Price breaks below key support → exit
✓Loss approaching $50 → exit, no averaging down
✓After 10:30 and momentum gone → exit
✓Log the trade immediately after exit

Mental rules
→Missed trade ≠ bad trader. The next setup is always coming.
→FOMO from one miss causes bad entries on the next setup. Reset between trades.
→Cutting a loss quickly is not a bad trade — it's discipline.
→Singles add up. Consistent wins build the account.
→The stock doesn't know you own it. It doesn't owe you a recovery.
→1% better every day. Compound the improvement, not just the money.
